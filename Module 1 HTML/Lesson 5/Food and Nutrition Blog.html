Importance of Fruits and Veggies

Fruits and vegetables are essential parts of a balanced diet because they give our bodies the energy needed to study, play, think, and grow. They provide important vitamins and minerals that keep the body healthy and strong.

Different colours in fruits and vegetables offer different nutrients. Eating a colourful variety every day helps support an active lifestyle and makes sure we get all the right benefits!

Power Up with Energy Foods

Energy foods like whole grains, oats, nuts, and seeds are super helpful for fueling our busy school days. They release energy slowly so we stay focused during classes and sports.

Choosing wholesome grains over junk food helps avoid sudden sugar crashes and keeps our minds sharp and ready!

Stay Hydrated with Healthy Drinks

Water is the best drink ever to keep our bodies cool, hydrated, and functioning properly. Fresh lime water and homemade fruit juices are also amazing choices packed with natural nutrients.

Avoid sugary fizzy sodas and drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your brain active and happy!
