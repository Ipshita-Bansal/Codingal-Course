💪 Why Your Body Needs Exercise

Your body is like a supercar — the more you use it, the stronger it gets! Kids who move for 60 minutes every day are happier, sleep better, and do better at school.

Dancing, swimming, football, yoga — anything that gets your heart beating faster counts. Keep moving and have fun!

🏃‍♀️ Active kids are healthier and happier every day!

☀ Quick Health Tips

  • 🍎 Eat 5 fruits and veggies every day
  • 💧 Drink 8 glasses of water daily
  • 😴 Sleep 9 to 11 hours every night
  • 🏃‍♂️ Move your body for 60 minutes
